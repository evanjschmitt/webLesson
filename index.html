Computer Science Lesson

This website will have three main functions:

  • The About Page defines what a loop and conditional statement are, and provides examples.
  • The People Page focuses on the functions of loops and arrays to dynamically make lists.
  • The Contact Page focuses on conditional statements and form verification.

This site serves as a very basic example of these two elements. With more advanced systems (PHP, MySQL, etc) you can complete a wider variety of tasks with these simple building blocks.
